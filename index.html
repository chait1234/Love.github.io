Dear Ray,

There is something I don’t tell you nearly enough: I admire you so deeply. It’s not just because you’re breathtakingly beautiful, or because you’re sweet, or because you somehow look perfect in every single photo. It’s the way you move through this world. I admire how fiercely you care for people, and the grace and emotional maturity you show when things get difficult. But most of all, I admire how you love me.You wait for me when things get crazy, and you welcome my chaotic world into yours with nothing but open arms. You have become my ultimate safe haven, and out of all the things I love about you, that is the one I cherish the most.

When people speak of love stories, they usually talk about the grand, sweeping moments. But when I think about us, I find myself back on those school walks, remembering the conversations that lasted far longer than they needed to, simply because I didn't want to leave your side.

Even back in Class 10, my eyes were constantly searching for you; every second minute, I was looking for your face in the crowd. I realize now that you became my comfort long before I even had a name for what I was feeling.

Thank you for every word we’ve shared, for your endless patience, and for the quiet kindness you show me every day. Above all, thank you for staying. You were my sanctuary then, and you are my home now.

Love, Chaitanya
